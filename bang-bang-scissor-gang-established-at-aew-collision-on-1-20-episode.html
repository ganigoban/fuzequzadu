Bang-Bang Scissor Gang Established at AEW Collision on 1/20 Episode

Publish date: 2024-09-05

On the latest episode of AEW Collision, the formation of the “Bang-Bang Scissor Gang” took place at the Chaffetz Arena in St. Louis, Missouri. This memorable moment occurred when the Bullet Club Gold trio, consisting of Jay White, Austin Gunn, and Colten Gunn, entered the ring with their newly won ROH Six-Man Tag-Team Championships and a cut-out of Juice Robinson to celebrate their victory.

As they celebrated in the ring, the AEW Trios Champions, The Acclaimed, and “Daddy Ass” Billy Gunn, also made their way to the ring. They praised the Bang-Bang Gang and emphasized the potential of joining forces.

After some discussion among the wrestlers, Jay White, Austin Gunn, and Colten Gunn made their alliance official by performing the scissor hand shake and the two sweet hand symbol, officially forming the “Bang-Bang Scissor Gang.”

This unique and entertaining moment marked the beginning of a new alliance within AEW, and fans can look forward to seeing how the Bang-Bang Scissor Gang’s storyline develops in the future.
