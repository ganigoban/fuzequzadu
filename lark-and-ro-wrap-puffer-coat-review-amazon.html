Outerwear trends have come and gone, but there's one classic, cozy silhouette that'll never go out of style: the puffer jacket. With a lofty, marshmallow-like fill, the sporty winter staple is supremely warm, and unlike your wool peacoat, its outer moisture-wicking material stands sturdy against rain and snow. But as much as puffer jackets reign supreme in cold weather, they tend to be bulky and boxy. Most lack a flattering fit, making them the coat of choice for extra-chilly days and casual wear.

If you're looking for a happy marriage between the two, turn to the Lark & Ro Short Wrap Puffer Coat that's currently up to 72 percent off on Amazon, with prices starting at just $23. Shoppers call it a "fashionable take on the puffer coat" and say it's "very stylish and warm." After trying it myself, I happen to agree.
