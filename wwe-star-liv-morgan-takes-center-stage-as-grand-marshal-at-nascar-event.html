WWE Star Liv Morgan Takes Center Stage as Grand Marshal at NASCAR Event

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Liv Morgan worked hard to establish herself as one of the most beloved Superstars in the WWE women’s division over the past couple of years. She is currently on the shelf due to injury and in the meantime, Morgan fulfilled her duties as the Grand Marshall at a recent NACSAR event.

Liv Morgan hasn’t had the best of luck this year as she suffered a shoulder injury a few months ago. Just when she came back, Morgan suffered yet another shoulder injury and it is believed to be serious one that will put her out of action for long time.

Liv Morgan previously announced that she would be she would be taking on the responsibility of Grand Marshal for Sunday’s Bank of America ROVAL™ 400 at Charlotte Motor Speedway.

The former SmackDown Women’s Champion was in high spirits as the Grand Marshall, as she said ‘Drivers, start your engines!’ with as much gusto and power as she could muster.

Fans were certainly glad to see her as the Grand Marshall as they felt she did a remarkable job as well. Liv Morgan is also getting back into shape as she is determined to return to the ring as soon as possible. We’ll have to see when she will be able to make her triumphant return to the squared circle.
