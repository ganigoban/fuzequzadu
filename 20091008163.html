Jim Carrey - Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-20

The Canadian-American actor was born in Ontario and is best known for starring in The Mask, The Truman Show, Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, and more

    Jim Carrey's Early Life

    James Eugene Carrey was born on January 17, 1962 in the Toronto suburb of Newmarket, Ontario, Canada. His family suffered from financial struggles, leaving them homeless at one point. However, once their situation began improving, he began devoting more time to his comedy, which began with a love for impressions.

    Carrey's father drove him to his first stand-up experience in Toronto at the age of 15, which bombed. However, he began developing his impressionist act further, adding more to his roster and eventually becoming a local success. He landed his first acting role in 1981 with the made-for-TV CBC movie .

    His work on screen slowed down during the late 2010s, although he won acclaim and another Golden Globe nod for his lead turn in the Showtime series (2018, 2020), and his biggest box office hits as Dr. Robotnik in the franchise (starting in 2020). Carrey has indicated in recent years that he intends to take time off from being on screen or retire altogether.

    Jim Carrey's Personal Life

    Throughout his life in the spotlight, Carrey has been involved in several high profile relationships, including with Linda Ronstadt (1983), January Jones (2002), and Ginger Gonzaga (2019). He was engaged to his co-star Renée Zellweger from 1999-2000 and dated makeup artist Cathriona White from 2012 to her death in 2015.

    Carrey married actress and Comedy Store waitress Melissa Womer in 1987, and they welcomed their daughter Jane later that year. They split up in 1995, and a year later, he married his co-star Lauren Holly, which lasted less than a year. His other most significant relationship was with model and actress Jenny McCarthy, with the pair dating from 2005-10.
