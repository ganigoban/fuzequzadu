Blake Lively Looks Incredible in Red Bikini and Heart-Shaped Sunglasses

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Unlike some of us (me), Blake Lively is out here thriving in the summer heat—and just casually dropped a photo of herself in a hot-red bikini and heart-shaped sunglasses to Instagram.

Kay, first thing's first: here are these, since we all need a pair immediately.

YooThink Love Heart Shaped Sunglasses

Meanwhile, Blake's comments section is active to say the least. One person seems to have tagged her trainer Don Saladino in response to the question "How have you had 4 kids" and Blake jokingly shot back "Wait. No. That's not how. He's not the father. He’s just the one who helps me fit into (some of) my clothes again after. He’s an even better person and friend than trainer. And that’s saying a lot. ♥️"

Back in 2022, Blake told Vogue “I think having children for me made me feel so much more in my skin. I never felt more myself or at ease in my own body or more confident — not to say that there aren’t a bevy of insecurities coming at me a million times a day, but I just feel incredibly settled.”

She added, "Whenever I create something, I create something with family in mind, because that’s just how I live. That also goes into the types of films I want to be a part of. I want to be in films, I want to make films, I want to author films that I would be proud for my children to see. Even the gowns I wear—I just want my kids to be proud.”

S'cute!
