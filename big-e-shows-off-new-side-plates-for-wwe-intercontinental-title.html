Big E Shows Off New Side Plates For WWE Intercontinental Title

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Big E is WWE Intercontinental Champion, and that came with a bit of customization for the belt. WWE had side plates made, but they have yet to give the title a good close-up shot.

One fan tweeted out saying that he will send $5 to the first person who can get him a good shot of Big E’s side plates. Little did he expect, but tagging Big E in that tweet actually paid off.

Big E didn’t reply with any words, but he did put out highly detailed photos of his Intercontinental Title side plates. He also didn’t include an address where that fan can send the five bucks.

Fans replied saying that this fan better get Big E his $5. Big E is a frugal man, no matter how much he loves the number in his bank account. Big E later replied to this fan to let him know that he can keep the $5.
