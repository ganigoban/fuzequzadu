Early Life

Vanessa, who was born on December 28, 1980, in Brooklyn, New York, was raised by her Italian-American family. Unfortunately, her father passed away when she was only two years old from a drug overdose, leaving her to be raised by her mother and stepfather, who owned a hair salon in New York.

Her childhood was challenging, but she was able to discover her interests and aspirations at an early age, which she has now accomplished.

The Ferlito family had been feeling discouraged for quite some time, but Vanessa’s mother made every effort to ensure her daughter received an education.

Career

Vanessa Ferlito is most renowned for her portrayal of Aiden Burn, a stunning forensic scientist with a sharp wit and fiery temper, on the popular show CSI: NY. She is highly dedicated to her job, meticulous in her approach, and known for her outgoing and charming personality.

Since her debut in the entertainment world in 2001, Vanessa has been featured in a plethora of films and TV shows. She has gained recognition for her performances as FBI Agent Charlie DeMarco in Graceland, and Claudia Hernandez in 24.

In the crime thriller “Shadowboxer” (2005), Vanessa Ferlito demonstrated her acting abilities alongside well-known actors. She brought depth to the comedic film “Man of the House” (2005) with her performance. In the sports drama “Gridiron Gang” (2006), Ferlito was able to showcase her versatility.

She also had the chance to explore the thriller genre in Quentin Tarantino’s “Death Proof” (2007). Ferlito’s comedic timing was highlighted in the Tyler Perry-produced “Madea Goes to Jail” (2009). She appeared alongside talented co-stars in the culinary comedy-drama “Julie & Julia” (2009).
