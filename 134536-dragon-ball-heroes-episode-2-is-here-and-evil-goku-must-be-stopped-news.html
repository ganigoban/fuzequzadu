&quot;Dragon Ball Heroes&quot; Episode 2 Is Here &amp; Evil Goku Must Be Stopped

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Although Dragon Ball Super has ended, Toei Animation has brought fans of the series a new show. The mini-series, entitled Dragon Ball Heroes, is a small promotional event, but it is all we have until another Dragon Ball show is created for the long run. The series is based around the popular video game and trading card series of the same name. The plot revolves around Future Trunks, who has been locked up on the Prison Planet and needs saving. In the latest episode of Dragon Ball Heroes, we find Goku in an evil state of mind. 

Goku and Vegeta are still on the Prison Planet trying to save Future Trunks when Evil Saiyan Cumber engages them in battle. Goku, being the hero that he is, attacks Cumber, but cannot make it through his ki. Instead, the ki turns Goku evil, and he engages in battle with Vegeta and Trunks. Frieza's sibling Cooler is also in attendance, and upon learning that his brother achieved the Golden Form, Cooler transforms as well. 

Golden Cooler is able to beat the Evil Goku when Vegeta and Trunks are defeated. Then, we get a surprise fuse that no one will complain about. The third episode of Dragon Ball Heroes is set to arrive on July 23. Check out episode two below.
