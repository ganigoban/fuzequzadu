Beyonc Wears Head-to-Toe Adidas Following Studio Session in NYC

Publish date: 2024-08-10
  • Beyoncé was photographed in New York City on Sunday wearing a head-to-toe Adidas ensemble following what was believed to be a photo shoot for an upcoming Adidas x Ivy Park collection drop.
  • The singer wore a striped jumpsuit, white sneakers, and a protective face mask.

Beyoncé is back to work.

The pop star and Black Is King creator was photographed leaving what was believed to be an hours-long photo shoot for an upcoming release from the singer's Adidas x Ivy Park collaboration. Wearing a black-and-gold, long-sleeved Adidas jumpsuit with her long, honey-highlighted hair flowing, Beyoncé sported a protective face mask and matching Velcro-wrap Adidas sneakers. She could also be seen snapping a casual selfie.

MEGA

Beyoncé first announced the relaunch of her renowned athleisure brand last spring, following the decision to part ways with British retailer Topshop, the brand's original business partner. The singer called her new joint business venture with the sportswear retailer the "partnership of a lifetime."

"Adidas has had tremendous success in pushing creative boundaries. We share a philosophy that puts creativity, growth and social responsibility at the forefront of business. I look forward to re-launching and expanding Ivy Park on a truly global scale with a proven, dynamic leader," Beyoncé previously said in a statement.

The new partnership was deemed an instant hit, with the entire collection selling out in mere hours following its release earlier this year.
