Producers For This Past Weeks WWE Smackdown Revealed

Publish date: 2024-08-18

This past week’s episode of Friday Night Smackdown was not necessarily well-received by fans. In fact, it even failed to break 2 million viewers. It seems there were some interesting names for producers during this week’s show.

According to Fightful Select, quite a few interesting names were producers for each other matches during this week’s episode of Friday Night Smackdown. This included Abyss & Petey Williams producing the tag team match between Los Lotharios and Cesaro & Mansoor.

Tyson Kidd and Molly Holly were responsible for producing Naomi vs. Shayna Baszler. The former Yung Dragons Jamie Noble and Jimmy Wang Yang produced Drew McIntyre vs. Ricochet, which was one of the best matches of the night for many.

Shawn Daivari was the producer for Happy Talk and Viking Raiders. Finally, Adam Pearce and Steve Corino were producers for Adam Pearce and Sami Zayn. We will have to wait and see who will produce next week’s Friday Night Smackdown.
