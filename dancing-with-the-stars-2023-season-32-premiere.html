Dancing with the Stars 2023 premiere recap: Spears family cameo, Kyle Richards, 'Scandoval' revenge

Publish date: 2024-09-05

He's Yours...

Singer-songwriter Jason Mraz and his partner Daniella Karagach take to the dancefloor with a floorwork-heavy cha-cha-cha to Jason's own tune, "I Feel Like Dancing," and this audience is going WILD!

The judges have spoken: Carrie Ann immediately deemed Jason's performance the "best of the night," Derek couldn't resist giving him a standing ovation, and Bruno was full of praise for his footwork.

And here are the scores: 7, 7, 7, a total of 21 out of 30, capping off what looked to be a triumphant debut for the Grammy winner!

Scoreboard check: Jason and Daniella currently lead the pack (21), followed by Xochitl and Val (18), Barry and Peta (16), Alyson and Sasha (13), and a tie for the bottom between Harry and Rylee plus Tyson and Jenna (12 each).
