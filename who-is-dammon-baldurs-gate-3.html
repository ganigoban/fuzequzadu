Who is Dammon baldurs Gate 3?

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Dammon is an NPC in Baldur’s Gate 3. He is one of the Tiefling Refugees hiding within the Druid Settlement at Silvanus’ Grove.

Is dammon in act 3 bg3?

Baldur’s Gate 3: Dammon Location (Act 3)

Baldur’s Gate 3 players that have made it to the game’s final Act should look for Dammon at the Forge of the Nine in the city of Baldur’s Gate. This building can be found on the east side of the city, and it is situated just west of the Elfsong Tavern.

What does dammon make with infernal iron?
