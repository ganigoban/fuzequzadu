When did Suzanne Somers pass away?

On Sunday, October 15, 2023, Suzanne passed away following a battle with cancer. Her publicist confirmed the news.

She passed away a day before her 77th birthday.

"A private family burial will take place this week, with a memorial to follow next month," her publicist said in a statement.
