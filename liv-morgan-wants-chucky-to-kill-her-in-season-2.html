Liv Morgan Wants Chucky To Kill Her In Season 2

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Chucky took over televisions once again, but this time it was in a new series. The Chucky revival was a hit and it’s only a matter of time before season two comes at fans. Liv Morgan wants to be a part of that.

Complex recently spoke to Liv Morgan where she opened up about her interest in getting involved in the Chucky show. She would love to be a part of season 2, even if it’s just to be another body for the demented doll to shove a knife in.

“Oh yeah, it’s something I thought about more recently than ever. I’m definitely interested. I feel whenever the timing’s right, the perfect opportunity will appear. Right now, I’m so focused on my work in WWE and winning that RAW Women’s Championship, but I definitely have goals for the future. I’d love to be in Season 2 of Chucky. I’d love to be killed off in a little cameo.”

We’ll have to see if Liv Morgan gets that cameo on Chucky. Her participation could certainly do a lot when it comes to getting interest on Chucky. Morgan might also find herself playing in a breakout performance down the line.
