Nicole Kidman Reveals She Was Engaged to Lenny Kravitz

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Back in 2015, Nicole Kidman blindsided us all with her so-close-but-totally-failed love story with Jimmy Fallon. Now, the actress is revealing more surprises about her love life.

While promoting her new HBO series Big Little Lies, Kidman revealed she was previously engaged to Lenny Kravitz. Yes, really. In an interview with Net-A-Porter's The Edit, the actress opened up about working with Zoe Kravitz on the new show, casually letting the bombshell slide out, "Well, I knew Zoe because I was engaged to her father. It's all in the family!"

Kidman and Kravitz reportedly dated back in 2003, after the actress's divorce from Tom Cruise and before she met current husband Keith Urban. Kidman added in the interview, "I love Lenny; he's a great guy."

The actress was spotted backstage at the 2013 CMT Awards with both husband Keith Urban and Kravitz (the three even posed together for a pic), so it looks like there are no hard feelings there.
