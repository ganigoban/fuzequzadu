Reese Witherspoon and Daughter Ava Phillippe Match in Strapless Dresses

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Ava Phillippe looked like a reflection of her famous mom last night, as the two attended a Tiffany & Co. fete in Beverly Hills.

Reese Witherspoon took her 24-year-old daughter as her date to the lavish affair at the Beverly Estate, hosted in celebration of the launch of the Blue Book 2024: Tiffany Céleste collection.

For the night out, the pair coordinated in chic strapless dresses. Witherspoon’s was a sleek black dress by David Koma with a high leg slit accentuated by a silky ribbon detail at the waist and a round crystal-encrusted clasp. She wore it with strappy black sandals and glittering jewelry from the luxury house, including big diamond studs, a large sparkly silver necklace, a matching cuff bracelet, and a ring. She wore her hair in a wavy side-parted ’do and had on bronzy makeup and a bright red pedicure.

Stefanie Keenan//Getty Images

Phillippe, meanwhile, picked a gray floor-length gown with a structured bodice, from Alberta Ferretti. She added some glamour to the monochrome look with a gold and diamond choker necklace and a matching ring from Tiffany & Co. Like her mom, Phillippe had on red polish, though hers was a deep crimson. She wore a glittery shadow and a glossy lip, and had her blonde hair styled in a wavy updo with strands framing her face.

They both carried black clutches.

Matt Winkelmeyer//Getty Images

Witherspoon, who recently split from husband Jim Toth, often brings her daughter to fashion events. In January, for example, the two made an appearance at Fendi’s haute couture Spring/Summer 2024 show as part of Paris Fashion Week, where they showed off two contrasting looks.
