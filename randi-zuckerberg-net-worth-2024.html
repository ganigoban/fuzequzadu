Early Life and Education

Randi Zuckerberg was born and raised in Westchester County, New York. She attended Harvard University, where she studied psychology and graduated in 2003. Her interest in technology was sparked during her time at Harvard, where she was exposed to the burgeoning tech scene.

Professional Career

Facebook Years

During her time at Facebook, Randi was instrumental in developing the company’s marketing strategies. She also played a key role in launching Facebook Live, a live streaming feature that has become a significant part of the social media platform.

Post-Facebook Career
