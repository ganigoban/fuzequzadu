Ten Fun Things to Do in Edina

Edina, a charming city located in Minnesota, offers a plethora of fun activities and attractions for both locals and tourists. From natural beauty to cultural experiences, this city has it all. If you’re looking for an exciting adventure or a relaxation spot, Edina won’t disappoint. Here are ten fun things to do in Edina:

Fishing at Centennial Lakes Park

For fishing enthusiasts, a visit to Centennial Lakes Park is a must. This urban park features a series of interconnected ponds that are stocked with various species of fish. Grab your fishing gear, cast your line, and enjoy the peaceful ambiance as you wait for a bite. Don’t forget to bring your camera to capture the beautiful surroundings!

Exploring the Edinborough Park

Edinborough Park offers an indoor oasis perfect for all ages. This expansive indoor park features a tropical-themed pool area, indoor playgrounds, a walking track, and even a fitness center. Kids can enjoy splashing around in the pool or climbing through the playground, while adults can stay active by taking a stroll on the walking track. It’s an ideal place to spend a rainy day or beat the winter chill.

Shopping at the Galleria

If you love to shop, a visit to the Galleria is a must. This upscale shopping center is home to a wide variety of unique boutiques, high-end fashion stores, and well-known brands. From clothing and accessories to home decor and beauty products, you can find it all here. After a successful shopping spree, treat yourself to a delicious meal at one of the many restaurants in the area.

Having a Picnic in Arneson Acres Park

Arneson Acres Park provides the perfect setting for a peaceful and picturesque picnic. This park boasts beautifully landscaped gardens and serene walking trails. Grab a blanket, pack a delicious picnic basket, and find a cozy spot to relax and soak in the natural beauty. Don’t forget to take a leisurely stroll through the gardens to admire the blooming flowers and plant varieties.

Playing Golf at Braemar Golf Course

Golf enthusiasts can satisfy their passion by visiting the renowned Braemar Golf Course. This picturesque course offers 27 holes of stunning fairways, challenging greens, and breathtaking views. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced golfer, Braemar Golf Course provides a memorable experience for all skill levels. After your game, unwind at the clubhouse restaurant and enjoy a refreshing drink or a delicious meal.

Visiting the Edina Art Center

The Edina Art Center showcases the talent and creativity of artists from the local community and beyond. Explore the various art galleries, featuring a wide range of mediums, including paintings, sculptures, ceramics, and photography. Attend art classes or workshops to learn and hone your artistic skills. It’s a great opportunity to immerse yourself in the vibrant art scene and appreciate local talent.

Hiking at Minnehaha Creek

Nature lovers can revel in the beauty of Minnehaha Creek and its surrounding parkland. This picturesque creek is a perfect spot for a leisurely hike or a refreshing walk. Immerse yourself in nature as you listen to the calming sounds of the flowing water and take in the scenic views. Don’t forget to bring your camera to capture the stunning landscapes and wildlife along the way.

Watching Live Performances at the Edina Performing Arts Center

The Edina Performing Arts Center is a hub for live theater, dance performances, and musical concerts. Check their schedule to catch a performance by talented local artists or renowned touring groups. Enjoy an evening of entertainment and immerse yourself in the vibrant arts community of Edina.

Exploring the Southdale Center

Southdale Center is the oldest enclosed shopping center in the United States and is a must-visit for shopaholics. This mall offers a wide selection of stores, ranging from popular brands to specialty shops. Indulge in some retail therapy, grab a bite to eat at the food court, or catch a movie at the on-site cinema. It’s a perfect destination for a day of shopping and entertainment.

Relaxing at The Westin Edina Galleria

Treat yourself to a luxurious experience at The Westin Edina Galleria. This upscale hotel offers a tranquil environment, modern amenities, and excellent service. Unwind in the spa, take a dip in the pool, or enjoy a workout in the fitness center. With its convenient location near the Galleria shopping center, you’ll have easy access to both relaxation and retail therapy.

In conclusion, Edina offers a wide range of fun activities and attractions for all interests. Whether you’re a nature lover, a shopping enthusiast, or an art aficionado, this vibrant city has something for everyone. So, pack your bags and immerse yourself in the exciting world of Edina!
