AEW Shows Off Gruesome Bloodbath In New Screenshot For Fight Forever Video Game

Publish date: 2024-08-27

AEW continues to grow as a pro wrestling company and so it wasn’t a surprise when they decided to expand in various outlets. The company also decided to dive deep into the video game industry as AEW previously announced video game for consoles was in the works. The game has seen a lot of progress since then, as AEW recently showed off a very brutal screenshot of the upcoming game.

As previously reported, the AEW video game will be released sooner than expected. The game will be called AEW: Fight Forever and will feature a loaded roster of talent as well as match variety that fans will love.

This includes the Lights Out Match as well. AEW took to Twitter and uploaded a bloody screenshot of a Lights Out Match featuring Britt Baker and Thunder Rosa. It also featured thumbtacks for good measure.

This #AEWFightForever reveal at #AEWFullGear is going to be LIGHTS OUT!

Tune in this Saturday, November 19th LIVE on PPV @ 8pm ET / 5pm PT across all major providers! #AEWGames

The game will drop on PS5, Xbox Series, PS4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch and PC, which will likely be this year. It remains to be seen whether the game will become a hit with fans and critics alike.
