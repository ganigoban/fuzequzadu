Bryan Danielson and Eddie Kingstons Intense Verbal Confrontation Erupts After 12/23 AEW Collision

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Bryan Danielson is truly a man who loves professional wrestling and knows no limits when it comes to giving his all for the business. He also had a heated promo war with Eddie Kingston after AEW Collision went off the air this week.

Bryan Danielson and Eddie Kingston are two very different kinds of stars, which is very apparent from their in-ring style as well as overall demeanour and promo abilities. They also do not like each other whatsoever.

Ringside News provided full coverage of AEW Collision this week. It was quite show in San Antonio. It saw Eddie Kingston and Andrade El Idolo battle in the main event. Kingston beat Idolo and will now square off against Bryan Danielson on Dynamite next week in a huge match for the Continental Classic.

After the show went off the air, Bryan Danielson and Eddie Kingston engaged in a war of words. Telling Bryan Danielson that he was amongst other things, the ‘King of the Bums’, Kingston couldn’t wait to beat up Danielson in the Blue League final. Danielson noted that “Eddie Kingston has never beaten me in his life,” and then simply repeated, “Eddie Kingston is a bum.”

We will have to wait and see who will end up winning the Blue League finals and finally face the Gold League final winner to determine the first-ever AEW Triple Crown Champion this month.
